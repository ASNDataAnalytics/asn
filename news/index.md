# Changelog

## asn 0.0.1

- [`get_labs()`](https://asndataanalytics.github.io/asn/reference/get_labs.md)
  was renamed to
  [`get_labels()`](https://asndataanalytics.github.io/asn/reference/get_labels.md)
  to avoid a namespace conflict with
  [`ggplot2::get_labs()`](https://ggplot2.tidyverse.org/reference/labs.html)
  introduced in ggplot2 version 3.5.2.
